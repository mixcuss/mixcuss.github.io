About Us




In the last decades push messaging or instant messaging developed rapidly on mobile devices. In recent times, the availability of affordable high-speed mobile data and smart devices has kept this market at a boiling point. Analyzing various aspects of existing platforms we shaped our idea in the form MixCuss. MixCuss is a Google Maps based instant messaging app that enables users to interact without sharing contact numbers.


Our Philosophy
In the ever-evolving world of technology, we strongly believe in the possibility of presenting digital communication in a way that adapts to our diverse world. Our best efforts are dedicated to making your digital life more secure and private. With MixCuss, you can interact freely with anyone from anywhere. Now, interaction is more entertaining than ever.
Our Motivation

This highlights a lack of confidence in existing platforms. Through our analysis, we identified privacy concerns as a key factor contributing to this disparity. Throughout the development process, our priority has always been user privacy. Such an approach, which aims to integrate enhanced user engagement with privacy, brought many challenges to the table. We believe digital technology should not restrict people from exploration due to privacy fears. Rather, it should add an extra dimension to their freedom.
Our Objective
We had a clear vision for our goals from the outset. Our aim is to provide a platform that excels in the following aspects. We are dedicated to achieving our objectives while maintaining high standards. Additionally, we remain steadfast in our commitment to our Privacy Policy and the law of the land.

Privacy
Our utmost priority and concern is privacy. To fulfill this commitment, we have introduced several distinguished features. On our platform, users' contact information is not accessible and cannot be shared without their knowledge. We have also introduced 'Z Connect' along with QR codes, allowing users to share contact information even through other platforms.

Accessibility
Following our philosophy and the motivation outlined above, we designed our user interface and features to enhance users' ability to discover others without compromising privacy. On our platform, the integrated Google Maps feature allows users to explore and connect beyond their contact lists. In this way, everyone becomes your neighbor.

Freedom of Choice
We have thoughtfully chosen an approach that stands in stark contrast to current trends. When it comes to the question of choice, we avoid intervening. To uphold this principle, we refrain from using artificial intelligence or any computer algorithms to offer curated friend suggestions for our benefit. We strongly believe that users should have the maximum possible freedom to make their own choices.

Transparency
We are dedicated to maintaining transparency in our revenue model and the usage of user information.

Comfort
We aimed to create a platform that welcomes people with diverse perspectives across age, gender, and demographics, ensuring everyone can interact with each other comfortably.
